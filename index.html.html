1. Business Overview

A remote-first staffing and consulting company specializing in IT, Data Science, SaaS, and Clinical Programming. Our vision is to reduce overhead by eliminating office rent and travel expenses, redirecting cost savings into competitive salaries and better recruitment technology.

  • Goal 1: Place at least two candidates with third-party clients by 2025.
  • Goal 2: Establish a strong vendor network.
  • Goal 3: Achieve profitability through lean operations.

2. Service Lines & Revenue

We focus on four primary areas with the following typical profit margins:

4. Initial Setup Costs

Key overheads include legal fees, portal subscriptions, and a US-based recruiter if needed.

  • Legal Fees: $10,000–$20,000/year
  • Portal Subscriptions: $5,000–$15,000/year
  • Recruiter Salary (US-based): $50,000–$70,000/year (Optional if offshoring to India)
  • Miscellaneous: $5,000–$10,000/year

5. Recruitment Pipeline

Our hiring models include C2C for vendor-based contracts, W2 for permanent roles, and H1B sponsorship where specialized skills are needed. We primarily use platforms like Dice, LinkedIn Recruiter, Benchfolks, and Indeed/Monster for sourcing candidates.

6. Legal & Taxes

Choose an LLC (pass-through taxation) or C-Corp (21% federal corporate tax) in the U.S. State taxes vary widely—from 0% (Nevada, Texas) to around 9% (New Jersey, Illinois, etc.). For Indian operations, consider GST and TDS, plus PF/ESI if applicable for employees.

7. Roles & Salaries

Current plan: Hire 1 US IT Recruiter and 1 HR in India.

  • US IT Recruiter (India-based): INR 50,000–70,000/month
  • HR (India-based): INR 30,000–40,000/month

Future roles include a Manager (INR 70k–100k), Marketing Executive (INR 40k–60k), and Account Manager (INR 50k–70k).

8. Conclusion

This comprehensive model outlines a remote-first approach to staffing, leveraging global talent and optimizing overhead. By focusing on IT, Data Science, SaaS, and Clinical Programming, the company aims to achieve steady profitability and scalable growth from 2025 onward.
