2. Business Overview

Type: Remote-first staffing and consulting company

Core Specialties:

  • IT Services
  • Data Science
  • SaaS Solutions
  • Clinical Programming

Vision: To be a trusted, remote-first staffing and consulting firm delivering top-tier talent in specialized domains, while maintaining competitive cost structures through minimized overhead.

Mission:

  1. Provide high-quality staffing solutions in niche areas (IT, Data Science, SaaS, Clinical).
  2. Ensure compliance with U.S. labor laws (W2, C2C, H1B) and Indian employment norms.
  3. Achieve profitability by optimizing remote operations and controlling overhead costs.

3. Team & Roles (Indian Salaries)

Currently, the plan is to hire two positions in India:

  1. US IT Recruiter (India)
    • Monthly Salary: INR 50,000 – INR 70,000
    • Key Responsibilities: Sourcing IT candidates for U.S. placements, handling vendor partnerships (C2C), and coordinating interviews.
  2. HR Candidate (India)
    • Monthly Salary: INR 30,000 – INR 40,000
    • Key Responsibilities: Internal HR policies, payroll coordination (India side), onboarding support, and compliance with Indian labor laws.

Note: Salaries are indicative and may vary based on individual experience and regional cost of living.

4. Future Role Estimates (Manager, Marketing, Account Manager)

As the company scales, additional roles may be required:

  1. Manager Role (Future)
    • Monthly Salary (Estimated): INR 70,000 – INR 90,000
    • Responsibilities: Oversee recruiter performance, strategic planning, client escalations, and high-level decision-making.
  2. Marketing Role (Future)
    • Monthly Salary (Estimated): INR 50,000 – INR 60,000
    • Responsibilities: Digital marketing campaigns (LinkedIn ads, SEO), brand building, and lead generation.
  3. Account Manager Role (Future)
    • Monthly Salary (Estimated): INR 45,000 – INR 55,000
    • Responsibilities: Manage client relationships, handle renewals/up-sells, and act as a liaison between recruiters and clients.

5. Hiring Plan

  1. Immediate Hiring
    • One (1) US IT Recruiter (India)
    • One (1) HR Candidate (India)
  2. Future Hiring (based on growth milestones)
    • Manager (when team size > 5 recruiters)
    • Marketing Specialist (when monthly placement revenue exceeds a certain threshold)
    • Account Manager (when managing over 5+ active clients simultaneously)

11. Legal, Compliance & Taxes

Key U.S. Compliance Requirements

Requirement Process Cost (USD)
H1B Sponsorship LCA, Form I-129, USCIS fees $3,000–$5,000/case
W2 Compliance Payroll taxes, benefits (healthcare) $2,000–$5,000/year
C2C Contracts Vendor credential checks, custom agreements $1,000–$2,000/year
Green Card Process PERM, I-140, I-485 $10,000–$15,000/case

Tax Considerations

  1. Corporate Structure
    • LLC/S-Corp: Pass-through taxation at individual rates (10%–37% + state)
    • C-Corp: ~21% federal corporate tax + applicable state corporate taxes
  2. Payroll Taxes
    • Deduct FICA (Social Security, Medicare), federal and state income taxes for W2 employees.
  3. State Taxes
    • Vary by state (see Annex below)
  4. Sales & Service Taxes
    • Many states do not tax staffing/consulting, but rules differ.

15. Recruitment Workflow & Incentives

Revenue & Profit Calculation

Stage Process Cost/Revenue Structure
Client Billing \$100–\$180/hour billed to the client Example: \$120/hr × 160 hrs/month = \$19,200/month
Candidate Pay \$60–\$100/hour paid to candidate Example: \$70/hr × 160 hrs/month = \$11,200/month
Profit Difference between client billing & candidate pay \$19,200 – \$11,200 = \$8,000/month profit
Recruiter Incentives 5%–10% of monthly profit \$8,000 × 5% = \$400/month

Incentive Structure

Monthly Incentives

  • Flat Incentive: \$400–\$800 per successfully onboarded candidate
  • Variable Incentive: 5%–10% of profit margin per candidate

Quarterly Review Incentives

17. Additional Considerations

  1. Initial Market Research
    • Study competitor pricing in IT, Data Science, Clinical staffing.
    • Validate demand in targeted U.S. regions.
  2. Financial Reserves
    • Maintain 2–3 months of candidate payroll and salaries in reserve.
    • Budget for recruitment platforms and unforeseen legal fees.
  3. Technology Stack
    • ATS: Bullhorn, JobDiva, Greenhouse
    • CRM: Salesforce, Zoho CRM
    • Sourcing Platforms: Dice, LinkedIn, Benchfolks
  4. Legal & Compliance Setup
    • Draft contracts (clients, vendors, employees).
    • Register for state/federal taxes, set up payroll for W2/1099 if needed in the U.S.
  5. Vendor & Client Onboarding
    • Standardized agreements outlining fees, milestones, SLAs.
    • Vetted vendor list with performance benchmarks.

18. Actionable Checklist

Pre-Launch

  • Register the business (LLC/S-Corp/C-Corp) in the U.S.
  • Open business bank accounts (India & U.S. as required).
  • Purchase recruitment platforms (Dice, LinkedIn Recruiter).
  • Hire 1 US IT Recruiter (India) & 1 HR Candidate (India).

Post-Launch

  • Source candidates in IT, Data Science, Clinical Programming.
  • Onboard clients via targeted outreach, vendor partnerships.
  • Track metrics (time-to-fill, cost-per-hire, candidate quality).
  • Optimize marketing and recruitment strategies continually.

19. Competitive Analysis

  • Pricing Advantage: Lower overhead (Indian operations) allows competitive rates in the U.S. market.

21. Financial Safety Nets & Scalability

Cash Flow Management

  • Invoice factoring: Turn pending invoices into immediate cash.
  • Short-term bank loans or lines of credit to manage payroll surges.

Scalability Plan

  • \$1M Annual Revenue: Add cybersecurity, DevOps staffing.
  • 6 Placements per Quarter: Hire additional recruiters.
  • \$2M Annual Revenue: Expand into AI/ML Engineering and Cloud Migration specialties.

22. Client Engagement & Retention

KPIs:

  • Net Promoter Score (NPS) target: 80+
  • Client Retention Rate: 90%
  • Placement Time: <30 Days

Upselling Opportunities:

  • Workforce planning consultation
  • Project-based consulting in data visualization, compliance audits

23. Enhanced Vendor Management

  1. Vendor Selection
    • Reliability: At least 95% on-time submission rate
    • Favorable payment terms (net-30 or net-45)
  2. Continuous Vetting
    • Quarterly performance reviews
    • Candidate feedback and compliance checks
  3. Emergency Backup Vendors
    • Maintain a list of 3–5 additional vendors in each specialty.
    • Transition quickly in case of vendor performance issues.

24. Risk Management & Contingency

  1. Compliance Monitoring
    • Keep track of H1B cap changes, immigration backlog updates.
    • Provide advisory to clients on compliance best practices.
  2. Market Diversification
    • Expand beyond core IT and Healthcare (FinTech, Cybersecurity).
    • Mitigate impact of a single industry’s slowdown.

25. Diversification Beyond Core Industries

  • AI/ML Engineering: Demand for predictive modeling and automation.
  • Cloud Migrations: Heightened adoption for modernization.
  • Cybersecurity: Ongoing shortage of skilled professionals.
